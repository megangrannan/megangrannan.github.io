What Are The Most Popular Topics On Data Science Stack Exchange?

August 12, 2020 in EDA, Analysis, Python

Imagine you are a company creating data science related courses and content - what new content should you add?

I analyze data from Data Science Stack Exchange and Cross Validated Stack Exchange to identify popular topics, track interest in these topics over time, and look for associations between popular tags. I query a database to get data, analyze the data using Python and related libraries, and include a variety of visualizations to better illustrate the findings.
